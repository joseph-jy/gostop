### Original Files Used

- Hwatu001.svg through Hwatu012.svg (Monthly card sets)
- Each monthly file contains 4 cards representing the 12 months of the year
- Total: 48 cards extracted and converted to PNG format

### Modifications

- Converted from SVG to PNG format
- Resized to 150x225 pixels for accessibility
- Individual cards extracted from monthly composite files
